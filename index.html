This is a middle-line, average DPS estimator. Your in-game parses will vary in comparison due to ATK vs AC, mob, etc.

Procs are not included in DPS.

Backstabs are not included in DPS.

Main Hand DPS is base. No double or triple attack. Off Hand DPS accounts for Dual Wield Chance.

Total DPS accounts for Dual Wield, Double Attack and Triple attack respectively (beta).

All level-based haste caps are in place, as well as class-based damage caps.

Skills are not tied to level caps so you have more freedom to play with odd skill levels.
